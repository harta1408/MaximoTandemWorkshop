# Autodesk Tandem + IBM Maximo Digital Twin Workshop

> **3-Day Hands-on Workshop**
> Digital Twin Implementation for Operations & Maintenance
> **This Workshop Conduct for Otorita Ibu Kota Negara (OIKN)**

---

## Overview

This workshop introduces **IBM Maximo Manage** and **Autodesk Tandem** as complementary platforms for Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) and Digital Twin implementation.

Participants will learn how IBM Maximo manages the complete maintenance lifecycle while Autodesk Tandem provides a visual Digital Twin that enables engineers to locate, understand, and manage physical assets within a 3D environment.

> **Workshop Theme**
>
> **Operate Assets with Maximo. Visualize Assets with Autodesk Tandem.**

---

## Target Audience

* Maintenance Engineers
* Asset Engineers
* Reliability Engineers
* Facility Managers
* Maintenance Supervisors
* IBM Maximo Administrators
* Digital Twin Administrators
* IT Administrators

---

## Learning Objectives

After completing this workshop, participants will be able to:

* Understand Enterprise Asset Management (EAM)
* Manage assets using IBM Maximo Manage
* Create and manage Work Orders
* Execute Preventive Maintenance
* Navigate Autodesk Tandem facilities
* Locate assets using the Digital Twin
* Manage equipment information inside Tandem
* Understand Digital Twin concepts for Operations & Maintenance
* Understand Autodesk Tandem integration with IBM Maximo
